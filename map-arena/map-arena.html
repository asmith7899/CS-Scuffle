How To Play

 ×

Your stats are shown in the upper left corner of the screen; watch your stamina as you fight!

Your opponents stats are shown in the upper right corner. Watch to see how your attacks affect them!

Use your arrow keys to move your player through the arena.

You can bump your opponent using the e key or pick up and throw objects at your opponent using the f key.

Use the instructional button in the top right corner of the stamina bar to access these instructions throughout the game.

You're ready to play - good luck!
