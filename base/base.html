How to Begin

Select two avatars from the roster of players on this screen.

The first avatar you select will be your character, and the second avatar will be your AI controlled opponent.

Choose a difficulty level from the menu. Easy is the default.

Continue selecting and de-selecting until you're happy with your choices.

When you're ready to begin, choose 'Start Game' from the menu on the left!

Welcome to CS Scuffle!

Instructions for how to play the game are below. Click anywhere outside the modal to close.

Game Transitions

Once you've chosen to begin the game, you will transition to the first gameplay arena.

As the game progresses, you will transition to new arenas.

These transitions are triggered by a few events - the timer running out, loss of a player's health, or destruction of all on-screen elements.

The timer is displayed at the top of the screen in game arenas, along with player health.

Game Elements

There are multiple game arenas to move through, and each arena contains different elements.

The most important elements are yourself and your opponent; these are the characters which will determine how the game progresses.

Other visual page elements, such as images or text blocks, can be used by players to inflict damage on their opponent.

Decreasing the health of all objects will earn you points and will progress you through different game arenas.

Character Movement

You can move your character via the arrow keys on your keyboard. Your goal is to bump or hit your opponent.

Your opponent has the same goal; they will pursue you and attempt to hit or bump your character. Be prepared to defend!
